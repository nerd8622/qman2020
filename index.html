The Story Of Q-Man

Q-Man stated that he "wanted to make the world a better place". Anyone who had the privilege of meeting him knows that he accomplished this. While a student at Highlands Ranch High School, Q participated in cross country and wrestling. He was known around school for being friends with everyone and his contagious positive attitude. The purpose of the Q-Man Classic was to preserve his legacy and continue to make the world a better place, just as Q would want.

Non Profit Information

The donations recieved from our race go towards Teaching the Autism Community Trades, their website can be found here. TACT works to help those with Autism learn important skills and trades to give them a greater chance of getting a job. They offer classes in auto mechanics, carpentry, electrical, welding, and computer science designed specfically for individuals on the autism spectrum. TACT offers these classes for free to their students and work to make the out of pocket costs minimal so that they can continue to support students and families that need it most.
